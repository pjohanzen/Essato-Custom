Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our custom tailoring services

How long does it take to get my clothes?

After we place your order, we normally have your clothing ready in 4 weeks.

What if I need my suit quickly?

While our standard lead time is 4 weeks, we can rush an order if needed. We can rush a suit in as low as 12-14 days if needed.

What should I expect during my first appointment?

During your first appointment, we'll discuss your style preferences, take detailed measurements, and help you select fabrics and customization options. This appointment typically takes about 45-60 minutes.

Do you offer alterations for existing garments?

We create new custom garments. If you're looking for an alterations shop, Fanny Green is 2 blocks away and is our go-to for all of our alterations work.

What's the difference between Custom and Bespoke?

Custom garments include one fitting with alterations covered. This produces a 95% great fit at a lower price.
Our Bespoke service offers unlimited fittings until the fit is exactly how you want it (100%).

Do you require a deposit?

We require full payment up front for your order (we have to pay our factory when we place your order).
